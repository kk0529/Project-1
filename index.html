Why The World At Large?

There's a lot of world out there; go find it! Our mission is to provide information to individuals about what different regions of the world look like in real time as well as what's going on in those parts of the world. We achieve this through the use of both article and video search technologies. We would like to thank both News API and webcams.travel API for allowing us to utilize their resources to further our mission. Above, you will see links to our article and video search. Once you sign up and agree to our terms of service and use, you will be able to access the articles and video feed! Go ahead and give it a try!
